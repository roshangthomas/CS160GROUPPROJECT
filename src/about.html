About Festival Artist Selection

We are a team of music lovers who want to help festival-goers find their perfect lineup. With our web-app, you can easily browse and select your favorite artists to create a custom lineup for your next festival experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for people to discover new music and have an unforgettable festival experience. We believe that music has the power to bring people together and create positive change in the world.
